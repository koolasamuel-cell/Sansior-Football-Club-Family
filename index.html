SANSIROFOOTBALL FAMILY

Established on 10th January 2000, SANSIRO FOOTBALL FAMILY is a vibrant community-based football group at Buokrom Estate / Asore Nkwanta / Ideal College. With over 120 members, we come together every Saturday to build unity, friendship, and lifelong support.

Our Identity

The name "SANSIRO" was inspired by the famous AC Milan and Inter Milan derby — a symbol of passion, intensity, and competition. Our family is divided into AC Milan and Inter Milan, competing in seasonal leagues.

Our Activities

We organize weekly football matches every Saturday. In the recently concluded 2025–2026 season, Inter Milan emerged champions.

Community & Values

We are more than football — we are family. We support members during important life events and operate as a self-sponsored group driven by unity, respect, and togetherness.

Achievements

Our journey includes winning 1 major trophy, finishing as runners-up 3 times, and producing 2 players who have featured in the U-17 National Team.

Leadership

The family is guided by our secretaries: Fosu (Oguamaa), Sarforo, Asibey, and Lampard, ensuring smooth organization and growth.

Honoring a Legend

We honor the memory of Coach Pepe, whose dedication played a vital role in building SANSIRO FOOTBALL FAMILY. His legacy continues to inspire us.
