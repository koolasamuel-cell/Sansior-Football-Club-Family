About Us

SANSIRO FOOTBALL FAMILY

Established on 10th January 2000, SANSIRO FOOTBALL FAMILY is a vibrant community-based football group located at Buokrom Estate / Sore Nkwanta. With over 120 members and no age limits, we come together every Saturday not just to play football, but to build unity, friendship, and lifelong support for one another.

Our Identity

The name “SANSIRO” was inspired by the famous AC Milan and Inter Milan derby — a symbol of passion, intensity, and competition. Today, our family is proudly divided into AC Milan and Inter Milan, competing in seasonal leagues that bring excitement and unity to our community.

Our Activities

We organize weekly football matches every Saturday, alongside seasonal competitions. In the recently concluded 2025–2026 season, Inter Milan emerged champions with [POINTS] points.

Community & Values

We are more than football — we are family. We support members during important life events such as weddings and funerals, and operate as a self-sponsored group driven by unity, respect, and togetherness.

Achievements

Our journey includes winning 1 major trophy, finishing as runners-up 3 times in community galas, and producing 2 players who have featured in the U-17 National Team.

Leadership

The family is guided by a dedicated team including our secretaries: Fosu (Oguamaa), Sarforo, Asibey, and Lampard, ensuring smooth organization and growth of the group.

Honoring a Legend

We honor the memory of Coach Pepe, whose dedication and leadership played a vital role in building and sustaining SANSIRO FOOTBALL FAMILY. His legacy continues to inspire us.
